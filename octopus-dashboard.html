🐙 Octopus Energy Dashboard

Your smart-meter usage, costs, live Agile prices, the cheapest times to use electricity, and a “would Agile save me money?” backtest — straight from the Octopus API, entirely in your browser.

Advanced: API base URL

🔒 Private by design. There is no server: your API key and account number are stored only in this browser’s localStorage and are sent only to api.octopus.energy. Nothing is uploaded or tracked anywhere else. Get your key from your Octopus account page.

Unofficial tool — not affiliated with Octopus Energy. All data stays in your browser. Prices shown include VAT.
